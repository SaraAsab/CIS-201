About yu Library

Al Yamamah University Library is a beacon of knowledge and information, providing a comprehensive educational and research environment that meets the needs of students and faculty members. The University Library encompasses one Male (Centra Library) and a Female Section Library. The library is designed according to the latest international standards to be a major center for academic resources, with a focus on diverse disciplines such as Business Administration, Engineering, Architecture, and Law. The library includes a wide collection of books, scientific journals, and databases in both Arabic and English. In addition, the library is equipped with the latest automation technologies, computers connected to the Internet, and a wireless network, allowing users to easily access the library’s catalogue, databases, and search engines available from inside and outside the campus via the university’s website. The library team works dedicatedly to provide academic support and advice, drawing on their diverse expertise to ensure that all members get the most out of library services. Whether you are a student, researcher, or faculty member, you will find in our library everything you need to achieve academic and research excellence.
